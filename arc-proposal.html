BRAND PROPOSAL

A bridge between two points.

The identity reduces to one primitive: a single arc held by two square endpoints. It reads as a smile, a bridge, and a visible connection without relying on restricted language.

Move from a word-led kindness identity to a shape-led connection identity.

The mark should not explain the mission. It should make the mission recognizable.

MARK ANATOMY

Human feeling, disciplined form.

01

The Arc

A smile at first read, a bridge at second read, and a horizon when scaled into environments.

02

The Square Points

Two solid endpoints preserve the architectural confidence of the old period mark and keep the symbol from becoming a generic face.

03

The Space Between

The open middle is the idea: one side begins an act, another side receives it, and the brand makes the connection visible.

Every act completes an arc.

IDENTITY SYSTEM

The mark can stay simple and still carry the whole world.

VOICE

Own connection, not virtue.

  • Wear the signal.
  • Meet in the middle.
  • Carry the arc forward.
  • Visible connection, made wearable.
  • One mark for what moves between us.

APPLICATIONS

Built for apparel, moments, and measurable participation.

Product

The mark works small, quiet, and desirable on caps, tees, hoodies, tags, patches, and team colorways.

Impact

Each completed arc can represent a recorded act, a local activation, a partner program, or a product-triggered contribution.

Experience

The same primitive can scale into walls, photo moments, event signage, school programs, and sports environments.

RECOMMENDATION

Lead with the arc. Let the name follow the symbol.

The mark gives the brand a protectable visual center while naming explores safer territory. It keeps the warmth of the current direction, removes dependency on restricted words, and creates a system that can grow across product, community, and commerce.
