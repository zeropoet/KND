OPERATIONS

The architecture can be iron-clad while the experience stays direct.

Archive

Every asset and drop can be versioned.

Dated product imagery, descriptions, colorways, bundle rules, and launch states are prepared to move into a formal archive as decisions lock.

Attestation

Each unit can carry its own record.

SKU, batch, material note, mark treatment, edition count, fulfillment state, and owner history can be attached when the shop becomes transactional.

Tracking

Status can become customer-facing only when useful.

Reserved, prepared, packed, handed to carrier, shipped, delivered, returned, and archived states can remain quiet until the client wants them visible.

Accounts

Sign-in can arrive without changing the shop shape.

Wishlist, order history, claimed units, team stores, family sizing, saved addresses, and collector records can plug into the same product IDs.
